ESG report: adopting science-based targets

Has economic uncertainty slowed progress towards net zero?
We looked at the views of 125 influencers in alternative investment funds (AIFs) to see progress made in adopting science-based targets (SBTs) given worsening economic conditions.
Our latest research reveals that the urgency among AIFs to adopt SBTs has dimmed since last year. But their long-term commitment to net zero is unchanged.
